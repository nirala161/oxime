Steps :

  1. click next on the bottom of current page and enter your name and post your video.
  2. Place your index finger on mobile's back camera and record a video of approx 20 second by turning on your flashlight.
  3. Don't choose the prerecoded file do current recording by choosing camcorder option.
  4. After submitting your video, wait for a time for uploading and processing. (may take couple of minutes depends upon file size)
  5. After uploading you will be redirected to result page.

Instruction :-

  • Don't move your finger during recording the video and don't keep space between finger and flashlight.
  • If your video size is higher than 50 MB then program will terminate automatically due to server storage limit.
  • Try to keep video size low as possible by setting up your recording quality for this purpose.
  • The result will depend upon the video you are posting.
  • The results from the developed application follow very closely with standard devices and this is valid for all the ranges of SpO2 values.
  • This application is alternative of oximeter, this is not replacable.
